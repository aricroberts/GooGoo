How it works

Four questions. One week, planned.

Tell goo-goo about your energy dips and your real schedule. It builds the rest — no wearables, no sensors, no account.

Your rhythm, mapped
Tell it when you hit an energy dip and it plans meals or snacks to get ahead of it — before it hits.
Cook or order — your call
Mark your busy days and goo-goo flips those meals to "order" automatically. Flip any single meal with one click.
Straight to your calendar
One click adds your whole week to Google or Apple Calendar — your own calendar app becomes the reminder.
A shopping list, sized for you
Scaled to your household and your food preferences — vegetarian, vegan, allergies, and more.
